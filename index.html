ABOUT US Winners' Chapel International Lomé
Winners’ Chapel International Lomé is a branch of the living faith church a.k.a Winners’ Chapel International.
The Liberation Mandate as delivered from God in an 18 hour long vision in May 1981 to the Apostle over this Commission is,
“And now,the hour has come to Liberate the World from all oppressions of the devil through the Preaching of the Word of Faith, and I am sending you to undertake this task.” Today, testimonies of liberation through our messages, books, tapes and other periodicals are most humbling. The Word of Faith is working like fire for the Liberation of Mankind across the nations of the world.
